Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Aadhaar card details for bidding verification
  • Property search preferences and requirements
  • Payment transaction IDs for ₹2,000 registration fee
  • Bidding activity and transaction records

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Verify bidder identity through Aadhaar authentication
  • Process property bidding transactions securely
  • Send updates about properties matching your requirements
  • Verify payment of ₹2,000 registration fee

3. Bidding & Payment

For live bidding participation, a registration fee of ₹2,000 is required. Customers must provide their transaction ID after payment. This fee is non-refundable and required before placing any bids. All bids are binding once submitted.

4. Data Security

All Aadhaar data and payment information is encrypted and stored securely.
